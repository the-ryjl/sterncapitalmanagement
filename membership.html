Membership Requirements

50% General membership constitutes attendance at 50% of SCM events and general meetings in a given semester.
  • Attend at least half of the events and general meetings hosted by SCM during the semester.
  • General meetings, speaker events, workshops, and other official SCM programming may count toward participation.
  • Students with questions about their membership status should reach out to SCM leadership at scm@stern.nyu.edu or speak with the team after a meeting.
