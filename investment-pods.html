SCM's student-led investment platform

Investment Pods

Three focused teams. One shared view of the markets. SCM's investment pods give students a collaborative setting to explore ideas, strengthen their research, and learn how different parts of the market connect. Interested? Email scm@stern.nyu.edu for updates on our Fall 2026 Pod Program!

Three lenses on the market

Focused coverage.
Broader perspective.

Each pod follows a distinct area of the market while contributing to a shared investment conversation. Together, they help members see how macro forces, sector trends, and company fundamentals shape opportunity.

FX

FX

Exploring currencies, global macro themes, and the market forces that connect economies and companies.

HC

Healthcare

Following innovation, industry dynamics, and the evolving businesses shaping health and life sciences.

TX

Tech

Studying the companies, products, and structural shifts driving the digital economy forward.

The pod experience

Learn by doing, together.

Pods are designed to make investment research approachable, collaborative, and connected to the real world. Members build fluency by following their area over time, discussing new developments, and sharing perspectives with the wider SCM community.

01

Explore

Develop curiosity about a market, sector, or company and learn which questions matter.

02

Analyze

Turn information into a clear point of view through thoughtful research and discussion.

03

Share

Bring ideas to the group, learn from feedback, and connect insights across all three pods.
